About Jeremy

A life devoted to worship and the Word

Jeremy Jeradi is a Kenyan minister of the Gospel — a preacher, revivalist, and worship leader dedicated to leading people into deeper intimacy with God through the preached Word, prayer, and worship.

Through revival meetings, worship gatherings, preaching engagements, and leadership mentorship, he inspires believers to pursue God's presence and live transformed lives. He is also a songwriter and gospel recording artist, carrying the same message he preaches into song, having released songs of blessing and worship such as Sijasahau and Baba Eh.

He is the founder of Worship & Prayer Camp (WP), leads The Shekinah Team, and serves as CEO of Great Nation Community, raising a spiritually empowered generation through worship, prayer, discipleship, and leadership development.

Mission & Vision

Four streams, one calling

01

Worship as a Lifestyle

Worship is more than music — it's a lifestyle of surrender, intimacy with God, and total consecration to Christ.

02

Revival

Stirring spiritual hunger, deeper prayer, and a fresh passion for Christ across the Church.

03

Prayer

Worship sessions that move into prolonged prayer and intercession, seeking God's presence above performance.

04

Raising Worshippers

Developing worshippers, not entertainers — carriers of God's presence, consecrated and Spirit-led.
